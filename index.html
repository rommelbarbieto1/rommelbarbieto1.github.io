SEO Techniques

Meta Descripitons: These techniques are short descriptions that show up below the title in search engines. They help users what to expect on the webpage and can influence click-through rates. Meta Descriptions help websites inprove their visibility and engagment among users. They provide a clear snapshot of the content, using keywords and clarity to enhance the visibilty so that users can click with ease. (Moz, 2023)

Backlinks: These are links from other websites that show your website. These are important because they help determine the credibility , authority and relevance on a website. Backlinks that are high in quality can improve the ranking of the webpage. When using backlinks, they require techniques such as content creating, blogging, etc. to encourage others to click to your site. Backlinks also help with fast indexing when search engines follow links to deiscover new pages. (Ahrefs, 2023)

Image optmization: This SMO technique is very important as it enhaces the performance of the website and their search engines rankings. It includes the compression of image to limit the file size so that speed times can load up faster and quality stays intact. Images that are optimized are especially important in mobile-first indexing, where companies such as Google uses to provide fast loading pages.

SMO Technique

Social Sharing Buttons: Social sharings buttons are a great feature as they provide encouragement to users who regularly use social media to share their content. Sharing buttons such as Facebook's "Like" button or X's "tweet" button help increase the potential of one's content to go viral. The feature reallty puts a great impact on users who use social media websites regularly. The only downside of using social sharing buttons is that they could potentially impact the speed of website loads so you must be careful when implementing them.

Instagram Integration: As many of you know, Instagram is one of the most popular social media applications in the world. Instragram's has implemented features where providing links to profiles or embedding feeds show a visual enhacment. Instragram strives to focus on visual content and this has allowed many users to confidently post thier own content using Instagram's Integration, which improves user engagment (Hootsuite, 2023)

Content Calender: The SMO technique is a tool for users who consistently post on social media platforms for engagement. Using this tool helps the user to plan to post content ahead of time, so that they can have a consistent schedule. This technique comes in handy as provides consistency, time efficiency, strategic posting, and collaboration (Hootsuite, 2023)
